About

"F I R S T"

FIRST, as “For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology” is an international youth organization that operates the FIRST Robotics Competition. Fist founded by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers in 1989, its expressed goal is to develop ways to inspire students in engineering and technology fields. Its philosophy is expressed by the organization as coopertition and gracious professionalism. FRC not only emphasizes the core principles of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), but also incorporates Arts to promote creativity, design, and communication, making it a holistic educational experience.

ROBOMANIA

“Imagine. Innovate. Inspire.”

Established in 2018 in Taichung City, Taiwan. Starting with just 13 members, we have grown to a diverse team of 123 members in total, representing various ages, genders, and nationalities.
