ABOUT "FIRST"

FIRST, as "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology" is an international youth organization that operates the FIRST Robotics Competition. Founded by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers in 1989, its expressed goal is to develop ways to inspire students in engineering and technology fields. Its philosophy is expressed by the organization as coopetition and gracious professionalism.

ROBOMANIA

Established in 2018 Taichung, Taiwan with just 13 members, Robomania have grown into a diverse team, representing various ages, genders, and nationalities. Our goal is to promote FIRST to students and the public in order to inspire individuals to learn more about STEAM.

FRC not only emphasizes the core principles of STEM, but also incorporates Arts to promote creativity, design, and communication, making it a holistic educational experience.
