About

FIRST, as "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology" is an international youth organization that operates the FIRST Robotics Competition.Founded by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers in 1989, its expressed goal is to develop ways to inspire students in engineering and technology fields. Its philosophy is expressed by the organization as coopetition and gracious professionalism.
