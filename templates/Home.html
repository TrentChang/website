About

FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) Team 7636 is a passionate and dedicated robotics team that participates in the global FIRST Robotics Competition. Team 7636 is made up of students, mentors, and volunteers who work together to design, build, and program robots for the annual FRC game challenge. Each year, FRC teams are given a set of game rules, and teams must build a robot to compete in matches, showcasing their problem-solving skills, engineering talent, and collaboration.
